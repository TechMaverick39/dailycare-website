Privacy Policy

At Daily Care Hub Nepal, we value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal information. This section describes what information we collect and how we use it.

Information Collected: When placing an order, we collect information necessary to fulfill your request, including your name, telephone contact number, delivery address, and shopping basket selections. When filling out our contact form, we collect your name, email, and message data.

Use of Data: The collected data is strictly used for order fulfillment, home delivery arrangements, customer service replies, and system performance optimizations. We do not sell, distribute, rent, or lease your personal information to third-party marketing companies.

Cookies & Tracking: We use analytical cookies (Google Analytics & Microsoft Clarity) to monitor website metrics, navigation drop-off points, and user behaviors to improve layout and shopping experiences.

Delivery & Shipping

We work to deliver your daily personal care essentials quickly and safely across Nepal.

Kathmandu Valley Delivery: Orders inside Kathmandu Valley (Kathmandu, Lalitpur, Bhaktapur) are delivered within 24 to 48 hours of order confirmation. Delivery charges inside the valley are Rs. 150, or completely FREE for orders totaling Rs. 2000 or above.

Outside Kathmandu Valley: Orders outside the valley are shipped via reliable local express courier networks. Shipping usually takes 2 to 4 business days. Prepayment is mandatory for all outside valley orders, and delivery fees are calculated based on weight and courier tariff rates.

Returns & Refunds

Please read our returns policy carefully, as hygiene and medical care items have strict sanitation requirements.

Return Timeframe: Customers can request returns or exchanges within 7 days of receiving their shipment.

Hygiene Exclusions: Due to the sanitary nature of our catalog (face masks, baby diapers, and sanitary pads), returns are only accepted if the items are completely unopened, unused, sealed, and in their original packaging. Opened packs cannot be returned or exchanged unless there is a confirmed manufacturing defect.

Defective Items: If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please notify us immediately via phone (9801915577) or WhatsApp. We will dispatch replacements free of delivery charges.
